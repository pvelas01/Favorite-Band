My Favorite Music

Genre

My favorite genre is Bohemian Latin American music.

Here are some reasons that I like Bohemian Latin American Music:

  • I love romantic Spanish music.
  • I like alternative sounds in Spanish that are feminist.
  • I like listening to women and queer women talk about their feelings and perspectives.

Favorite Bands/Musicians

My favorite artists are those who consistently create music that fills my soul. I love listening to Spanish music when I miss home, which is all the time. I feel like I'm in a place that is warm and full of life. I also like songs that are about heartbreak and the ups and downs of life. Latin American music can be very dualistic.

Here are my top five faves:

  1. Alice Wonder
  2. Rosalia
  3. Zahara
  4. Yahritza Y Su Esencia
  5. Ed Maverick
